How to Reach
By Road
Jahazpur is well connected by road. The nearest cities to the temple are Deoli and Bhilwara which have state transport and private buses running throughout the day. Bus connectivity during night is a bit sketchy. Details of the bus timings can be found on the RSRTC website.

There are some preferred taxi operators whose details with telephone numbers can be found on the contact page.

By Train
There is no direct train to Jahazpur but nearby cities are well connected by trains. The nearest railway stations to Jahazpur are Bhilwara, Kota and Indargarh. Here is a quick summary about the railway stations and respective distances. For more information, please refer Indian Railways website.
